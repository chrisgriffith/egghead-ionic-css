Kitty Hawk (CSM) | Antares (LM)

Apollo 14 was the eighth manned mission in the United States Apollo program, and the third to land on the Moon. Commander Alan Shepard, Command Module Pilot Stuart Roosa, and Lunar Module Pilot Edgar Mitchell launched on their nine-day mission on January 31, 1971.
